The odds of existing are slim,
So the odds of you and I existing at the same time must be next to impossible,
And I think that's beautiful,
Maybe the universe does work in my favour after all,
Because I got the chance to get to know you, your name, your favourite colour and every small thing about you.
It doesn't really change things much, but you made a difference in my life and that's what matters to me.
